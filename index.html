This visualization enables users to explore hurricane landfalls from 1851 to 2017. The area chart depicts the frequency of hurricane activities over year while the map shows the spatial distribution of hurricane landfalls.

Users can play around with the following interactions:
1. HOVER over the dots and CLICK to trigger path animations
2. CLICK the corresponding radio buttons to switch visualization techniques and DRAG sliders to play around with related parameters
3. CLICK the corresponding radio buttons to switch data sources or BRUSH the area chart at the bottom to change data input
